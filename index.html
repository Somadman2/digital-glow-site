About Us

The Digital Glow is a forward-thinking technology company founded in 2019 in Botswana. We are a multidisciplinary team of engineers, developers, designers, and strategic thinkers committed to delivering smart, sustainable solutions that empower growth and innovation.

Our expertise spans across:

  • IT Services: Automation, web & mobile development, e-commerce integrations, networking, and data protection.
  • Solar Solutions: Solar panel installation, battery storage, inverters, and custom energy systems for residential, commercial, and agricultural sectors.
  • Digital Transformation: Intelligent automation, AI tools, and streamlined operations tailored for modern businesses.

We combine affordability, agility, and technical precision to offer scalable results that transform the way our clients work and live.
